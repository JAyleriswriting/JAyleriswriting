Bio

Jasmine Ayler is writer of speculative fiction and romance novels with lead lesbian characters. She's a proud winner of three writing contests that were held at the West Des Moines Area Community College, scoring publication in their annually printed magazine Expressions each time for her short stories. Her future will hopefully continue to have creativity within it as she's currently studying game design to become the writer of video games. She's a dog mom and hobby photographer who loves spending time with her family and playing Xbox.
